# Strategy

## Current Strategic Priorities
- Stand up a small but functioning autonomous operating cadence.
- Focus on revenue creation before broad organizational expansion.
- Keep all work inspectable and easy to audit from the filesystem.
- Use written delegation and reporting to coordinate across roles.

## Near-Term Execution Logic
- Board sets focus.
- CEO converts focus into company priorities.
- Sales, marketing, research, finance, and operations create execution loops.
- Expansion happens only when current roles surface clear workload or capability gaps.
