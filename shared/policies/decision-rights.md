# Decision Rights

## Board
- Sets direction, strategic priorities, and board-level constraints.

## CEO
- Converts board direction into company-wide execution priorities.

## Managers
- Translate company priorities into department tasks and cadence.

## Individual Contributors
- Execute assigned work, surface signals, and escalate exceptions.

## Escalate When
- a decision changes pricing, strategy, or legal risk
- a blocker spans departments
- expected dependencies do not exist on disk
- performance is repeatedly off target without a clear local fix
